Let's talk something about me.

My name is Mebea Nibret. I was born in a small town called Gimja Bet in 1992 E.C.
When I was 5 years old, I joined a school called Horizon Academy, which is found in Bahir Dar city. I have learned their for 9 years since 2006 E.C.

I left Horizon Academy and joined Bahir Dar zuria secondary school when I became 9th grade. I have learned their till I became 11th grade. Then I left it and became a student of Bahir Dar secondary and preparatory school. I have learned for 2 years thier and have taken my university entrance exam. I scored 540 out of 700 in the exam and joined Addis Ababa University for Engineering faculity at Addis Ababa institute of Technology in 2011 E.C.

Now I am learning Software Engineering their. I'm 2nd year student.
This is my history overall.
